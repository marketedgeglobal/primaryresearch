# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free
experience for everyone who participates in this project.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and considerate.
- Assume good intent and communicate openly.
- Refrain from harassment, discrimination, or hostile behavior.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project spaces and public interactions related
to the project.
